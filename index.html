Welcome to the Scribe for Makers GitHub repository.

We've created a number of development tools that will make it easier for you to work with Scribe.

Python SDK

Our Python tools allow you to interact with your Scribe (Configure/Control/Query/Transfer)

Bluetooth low energy Frameworks for both iOS and Android

We provide BLE frameworks for both iOS and Android to make it easy for your apps to connect with your Scribe.

Reference Apps for both iOS and Android

You can use our Reference Apps to immediately begin collecting data, or as a starting point for your own application.
